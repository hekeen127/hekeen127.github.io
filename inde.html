Hi, I'm Hannah Keen and I Love Crocheting

Crochet is a process of creating fabric by interlocking loops of yarn, threads, or strands of other materials using a crochet hook. It is not hard to learn, and with this skill, you can make a lot.

What can you do?

Once you learn at only 3 to 4 basic crochet stitches, you can already make several items!

Crochet for Yourself

With this skill, you can make your own clothing, bags, stuffed animals, blankets, pillows, hats, and more. And you can make much of this with only knowing a few stitches. Crocheting can also help reduce stress and anxiety, increase productivity, and build self-esteem!

Creativity

Soon after learning how to crochet, you can start making your own patterns, such as new stuffed animals, fun sweaters, or intricate blankets. You can even eventually free crochet, where you have no pattern and just go at it, hoping to make something interesting in the end.

Community

Crocheting can help you connect to the community in several ways. One way is by joining crocheting/knitting groups to learn from others and have a place to relax. You can also give to the community with this skill, as many charities are centered around crocheted items, like Crochet for Cancer.
